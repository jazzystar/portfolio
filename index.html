Bio/Background

Hello! I am a third year Gaming Interactive Media Mobile student at Boise State University. I have been working on building and expanding my skills to go into media development. I am also working towards graduating with an ITM minor and am a part of the Honors College.

My major passion is game design, and I hope to one day work in Game Development.
